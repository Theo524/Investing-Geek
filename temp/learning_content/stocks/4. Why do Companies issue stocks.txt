Corporations issue stock to raise money for growth and expansion. To raise money, corporations will issue stocks by selling off a percentage of profits in a company.
There are many reasons that a company would issue stock to raise money.

Some of the common reasons include:

	- The development of new products

	- To purchase equipment

	- To buy new buildings

	- To increase inventory

 	- To expand and grow staff

	- To reduce debt

	- To prepare for a merger or acquisition

 	- To improve the value of a company

 	- To provide for greater flexibility
